About Enock Finance

Founder & CEO

Enock Nyameyie

Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Enock Finance. He is committed to building a reliable financial institution that supports individuals and businesses with accessible financial solutions.

Who We Are

Enock Finance is a fast-growing microfinance institution dedicated to providing secure, affordable, and flexible financial services. We empower individuals, entrepreneurs, and small businesses to achieve financial stability and growth.

Our Mission

To provide accessible financial services that promote economic growth, financial independence, and improved living standards.

Our Vision

To become one of the most trusted and customer-focused microfinance institutions in Africa.
